Things to know about Idaho

  • Soda Springs in home to the world's only captive geyser. It was discovered 80 years ago while drilling for a swimming pool.

  • Preston was the site of the 1863 Bear River Massacre. A monument was erected there to commemorate the event.

  • Fish Haven was originally named Rush Creek but the name was changed because of the proximity to Bear Lake and the abundent fishing there.
